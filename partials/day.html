Nestled in KK Nagar, Madurai, Cake Forest began with a simple belief: that every occasion deserves something extraordinary. From the first bite, you'll taste the care that goes into every layer.

We bake everything fresh daily, using quality ingredients to craft cakes, pastries, and desserts that carry the warmth of a home kitchen and the artistry of a professional bakery.
